The "new normal" and remote work

During COVID times most of us changed our way of working.

Companies had to allow remote working and in most cases, business went on as usual, if not better.

A lot of people changed jobs and started working at new places, sometimes too far away of their homes.

Now that the pandemic is hopefully ending, some companies accepted remote work as something to stay and others are eager to return to the office.

What will you do about it?

This survey ran from 14th to 16th of February and got the answers of 300 professionals, mostly from IT.

My name is Andre Carlucci, I hope you enjoy the results.

Meaning of some terms:

  • 100% Remote: Professional works remotely. Office is entirely optional or not available.
  • Remote Flex: Professional works most of the time remotely, but it is mandatory to go to the office one or two times per week.
  • 100% Office: Professional works in the office. Remote work is allowed only in very rare exceptions.
  • Office Flex: Professional works in the office but it is allowed to work from home one or two times per week.
